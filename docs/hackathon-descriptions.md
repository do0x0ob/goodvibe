# 黑客松頁面描述 / Hackathon Page Description

## 中文

**GoodVibe** — 在 Sui 與 Stable Layer 上打造的公益募資平台。你存入 USDC、鑄造心儀項目的品牌穩定幣，等於用穩定幣的收益支持公益——不必動用本金，資產始終在你錢包裡、流動性不鎖死，收益由項目方領取。項目方可發布進度、導出捐贈者名單，方便日後做追蹤或延伸功能。

---

## English (Short)

**GoodVibe** — A public-good fundraising platform on Sui and Stable Layer. Deposit USDC to mint project-branded stablecoins and your yield goes to the cause—no need to touch your principal; assets stay in your wallet and stay liquid. Project owners claim the yield, post updates, and can export donor lists for optional tracking or future features.
